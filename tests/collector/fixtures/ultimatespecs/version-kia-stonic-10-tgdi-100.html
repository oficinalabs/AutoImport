How fast is a Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100?
The Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 can reach 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) in 11.3 seconds and has a top speed of 179 km/h / 111 mph.

How many horsepower (hp) does a 2024 Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 have?
The 2024 Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 has 100 PS / 99 bhp / 74 kW.

How much does a Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 weighs?
The Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 weighs 1195 Kg / 2635 lbs.

What is the Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 gas mileage?
The Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 gas mileage is 6.9 L/100 km / 41 MPG UK / 34 MPG US Low WLTP / 5.2 L/100 km / 54 MPG UK / 45 MPG US Medium WLTP / 4.7 L/100 km / 60 MPG UK / 50 MPG US High WLTP / 5.8 L/100 km / 49 MPG UK / 41 MPG US Extra high WLTP / 5.5 L/100 km / 51 MPG UK / 43 MPG US Combined WLTP.

What engine is in Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100?
The Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 has a Inline 3, Petrol engine with 998 cm3 / 60.9 cu-in capacity.

What are the dimensions of the Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100?
The Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 is 414.0 cm / 162.99 inches long, 176.0 cm / 69.29 inches wide, and 150.5 cm / 59.25 inches tall, with a wheelbase of 258.0 cm / 101.57 inches.

How much cargo space does a Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 have?
The Kia Stonic 2021 1.0 T-GDI 100 offers 352 L / 12.4 cu-ft of trunk space, which expands to 1155 L / 40.8 cu-ft when the rear seats are folded.
